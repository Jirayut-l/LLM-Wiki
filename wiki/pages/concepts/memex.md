# Memex

The **Memex** is a hypothetical electromechanical device described by [[vannevar-bush|Vannevar Bush]] in his 1945 essay "As We May Think". 

It was envisioned as a personal library and communication tool that would allow users to store and retrieve information through "associative trails" rather than traditional hierarchical indexing.

The [[llm-wiki-concept|LLM Wiki]] pattern is a modern implementation of the Memex vision, where the LLM acts as the maintainer of these trails.
