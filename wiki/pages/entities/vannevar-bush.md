# Vannevar Bush

**Vannevar Bush** (1890–1974) was an American engineer, inventor, and science administrator.

He is best known in the context of information science for his 1945 essay "As We May Think," where he proposed the [[memex|Memex]], a precursor to the World Wide Web and modern knowledge management systems like the [[llm-wiki-concept|LLM Wiki]].
