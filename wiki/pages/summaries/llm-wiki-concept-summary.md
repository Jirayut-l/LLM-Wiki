# LLM Wiki Concept Summary

The **LLM Wiki** is a pattern for creating a persistent, compounding knowledge base. Unlike standard [[rag|RAG]] systems, it accumulates synthesis and cross-references over time.

## Core Architecture
- **[[raw-sources|Raw Sources]]**: The ground truth.
- **[[the-wiki|The Wiki]]**: The evolving synthesis.
- **[[schema|Schema (GEMINI.md)]]**: The "program" for the agent.

## Strategic Value
- **Persistence**: Knowledge is compiled once, not re-derived.
- **Maintenance**: The LLM handles the "grunt work" of bookkeeping and cross-referencing.
- **Associative Trails**: Inspired by [[vannevar-bush|Vannevar Bush]]'s [[memex|Memex]].
